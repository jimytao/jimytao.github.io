# 雅思 Task 1 — 全图表类型套用模板手册

> **使用说明**：`[X]` = 填入具体内容。每套模板结构为 **Introduction → Overview → Body 1 → Body 2**，字数目标 **150–180词**。

---

## ✦ 模板零号：数据变化句库（所有图表通用）

> 先掌握这张表，后面所有模板都是在此基础上拼装。

### 【大幅上升】

```
[A] surged / soared from [X] to [Y] over the period, reaching a peak of [Y] by [year].
[A] experienced a dramatic increase, climbing from [X] to [Y] — effectively doubling / tripling the initial figure.
[A] rose sharply, growing by [Z]% throughout the decade.
```

### 【小幅上升】

```
[A] saw a modest / marginal increase from [X] to [Y] over the period.
[A] grew slightly, rising from [X] to [Y], a gain of just [Z] units.
[A] edged upward from [X] to [Y], remaining relatively stable throughout.
```

### 【大幅下降】

```
[A] plummeted / dropped sharply from [X] to [Y], falling by approximately [Z]%.
[A] experienced a steep decline, decreasing from [X] to a low of [Y] by [year].
[A] collapsed from [X] to [Y], losing more than half its value over the period.
```

### 【小幅下降】

```
[A] fell slightly from [X] to [Y], a marginal decrease of just [Z] units.
[A] dipped from [X] to [Y], though it remained relatively high throughout.
[A] declined modestly over the period, ending at [Y].
```

### 【保持平稳 / 轻微波动】

```
[A] remained relatively stable / constant at around [X] throughout the period.
[A] plateaued at approximately [X] for most of the decade.
[A] fluctuated between [X] and [Y] with no clear overall trend.
```

### 【反弹 / 先降后升】

```
After falling to a low of [X] in [year], [A] rebounded / recovered to reach [Y] by [year].
[A] dipped to [X] in [mid-year] before recovering steadily to [Y] by the end of the period.
```

### 【大差距】

```
[A] significantly outpaced [B], with a gap of [Z] between them throughout the period.
[A] was considerably higher than [B], standing at [X] compared to just [Y].
In stark contrast, [B] accounted for only [Y], far below [A]'s figure of [X].
```

### 【小差距】

```
The gap between [A] and [B] was narrow, with [A] at [X] and [B] at [Y].
[A] and [B] showed similar figures, at [X] and [Y] respectively, with only a marginal difference.
[A] slightly outperformed [B], with a difference of less than [Z] units.
```

### 【超越 / 反超】

```
This trend continued until [year], when [A] finally overtook / surpassed [B].
By [year], [A] had overtaken [B] to become the [largest / most popular] category.
[A] and [B] crossed in [year], after which [A] took the lead.
```

### 【倍数关系】

```
By [year], [A] was approximately [X] times higher than [B].
The figure for [A] was double / triple / quadruple that of [B] by the end of the period.
[A] was [X]-fold higher than [B], standing at [Y] compared to just [Z].
There were [X] times as many [A] as [B] by [year].
```

### 【最高 / 最低起点】

```
[A] started the period at [X], representing the highest / lowest figure across all categories.
[A] began at a modest base of [X], the smallest of all groups recorded.
```

## ✦ 模板一：折线图 / Line Graph

> **适用**：随时间变化的趋势，通常跨越5–30年。

### Introduction

```
The line graph illustrates [the changes in / trends in] [topic] [in/between/from X to Y].
```

**示例：**
> The line graph illustrates changes in the number of international students enrolled in universities in three countries between 2000 and 2020.

---

### Overview（不写数据，只写总趋势）

```
Overall, [A] experienced the most significant growth over the period, while [B] showed a consistent decline. 
The most notable feature is that [A] overtook [B] in [year], reversing the initial pattern.

/ 若无交叉：
Overall, [A] remained the dominant category throughout, while [B] consistently recorded the lowest figures.
```

---

### Body 1（主要趋势组）

```
In [start year], [A] stood at [X], the highest / lowest of all categories. Over the following [Z] years, 
it [rose sharply / grew steadily / fluctuated] to reach [Y] by [end year]. [B], by contrast, 
started at [X2] and [trend description], ending the period at [Y2].
```

---

### Body 2（次要趋势 + 特殊点）

```
Regarding [C], the figure started at [X3] and remained relatively stable until [mid-year], 
before [rising sharply / plummeting] to [Y3] in [end year]. A notable exception was [year], 
when [unusual event / reversal described].
```

---

## ✦ 模板二：柱状图 / Bar Chart

> **适用**：类别对比，可能有时间维度也可能没有。

### Introduction

**（静态——无时间变化）**
```
The bar chart compares [the amount / level / proportion] of [topic] across [X] [countries / categories] in [year].
```

**（动态——有时间变化）**
```
The bar chart illustrates changes in [topic] in [place] between [year1] and [year2].
```

---

### Overview

```
Overall, [A] recorded the highest figures throughout, while [C] consistently showed the lowest levels. 
/ The most striking feature is the [increase / decrease] in [A] between [year1] and [year2].
```

---

### Body 1（最大 → 次大）

```
[A] accounted for the largest share, at [X%] in [year1], [rising to / falling to] [Y%] by [year2]. 
[B] followed closely, with [X2], though it [trend]. By contrast, [C] recorded [X3], 
[slightly lower than / nearly double] the figure for [D].
```

---

### Body 2（较小值 + 差距描述）

```
At the other end of the scale, [D] recorded the lowest figure at [X4]. 
The gap between [A] and [D] was considerable / narrow, standing at [Z] units.
[E] and [F] showed comparable figures, at [X5] and [X6] respectively.
```

---

## ✦ 模板三：饼图 / Pie Chart

> **核心词**：breakdown, proportion, share, distribution, accounted for, made up

### Introduction

```
The pie chart presents the breakdown of [topic] by [category] in [year / place].
```

**示例：**
> The pie chart presents the breakdown of household energy expenditure by type in the UK in 2020.

---

### Overview

```
Overall, [A] constituted the largest share of [topic], while [B] and [C] together accounted for 
the majority / a relatively small proportion of the total.
```

---

### Body 1（最大 → 次大份额）

```
[A] was the dominant category, accounting for [X%] of the total. [B] followed as the second largest 
contributor, making up [Y%]. Together, [A] and [B] comprised over half / [Z%] of all [topic].
```

---

### Body 2（小份额 + 综合描述）

```
The remaining [Z%] was distributed among [C], [D], and [E], at [x1%], [x2%], and [x3%] respectively. 
Of these, [C] was the most prominent, while [E] represented the smallest proportion at just [x3%].
```

---

### ⚠️ 饼图多年对比版本（两个饼图）

**Overview addition:**
```
Comparing the two years, the most notable shift was the [rise / fall] in [A]'s share, 
from [X%] in [year1] to [Y%] in [year2], while [B] experienced the opposite trend.
```

---

## ✦ 模板四：表格 / Table

> **核心词**：operating statistics, key data, ridership, in terms of, figures for

### Introduction

```
The table compares [the key data / operating statistics of] [X] [subject] in terms of [col1], [col2], and [col3].
```

**示例：**
> The table compares six subway networks in terms of their age, total route length, and annual ridership.

---

### Overview

```
Overall, [A] recorded the highest figures across most categories, while [B] was notable for 
[its exceptionally low / high] [specific column]. There is a clear correlation between [col1] and [col2].
```

---

### Body 1（行与行对比——最突出的2–3行）

```
[A] stood out as the [largest / oldest / most extensive], with [col1 = X], [col2 = Y], and [col3 = Z]. 
In contrast, [B], while [younger / smaller], recorded a [surprisingly high / low] figure for [col], at [N].
```

---

### Body 2（剩余数据 + 横向规律）

```
The remaining [subjects] showed more moderate figures. [C] and [D] were broadly similar, 
with [col] of [X] and [Y] respectively. Regarding [col3], the figures ranged from [min] to [max], 
suggesting [brief interpretation].
```

## ✦ 模板五：地图题 / Map

> **核心词**：development, transformation, layout, replaced by, demolished, constructed, expanded, relocated

### Introduction

**（两张地图对比）**
```
The maps illustrate the [development / transformation] of [place] between [year1] and [year2].
```

---

### Overview

```
Overall, the area underwent significant development / change over the period. 
The most notable transformation was the [replacement of / addition of / removal of] [feature], 
while the [eastern / northern] part of the area remained largely unchanged.
```

---

### Body 1（主要变化区域）

```
In [year1], the [area] was [dominated by / covered with] [original features]. By [year2], 
this had been replaced by / transformed into [new features]. 
[A specific building / road] was [constructed / demolished / relocated / expanded] to [location/purpose].
```

---

### Body 2（次要变化 + 未变区域）

```
To the [north/south/east/west], [B feature] was [added / removed / extended]. 
In contrast, the [area] in the [direction] remained largely unaltered, retaining its original [layout / function].
A new [road / park / residential area] was introduced [in the centre / along the southern boundary].
```

---

### 地图常用方位 + 变化词快查

| 方位描述 | 变化描述 |
|----------|----------|
| to the north/south/east/west of | was replaced by |
| in the centre / central area | was demolished / knocked down |
| along the northern boundary | was constructed / built |
| adjacent to / next to | was converted into |
| surrounding the [area] | was extended / expanded |
| in the [top-left / bottom-right] corner | was relocated to |

## ✦ 模板六：流程图 / Process Diagram

> **核心词**：stage, step, subsequently, following this, finally, is produced / generated / treated

### Introduction

**（线性流程）**
```
The diagram illustrates the stages involved in [the production / manufacture / treatment] of [product].
```

**（循环流程）**
```
The diagram depicts the cyclical process of [topic], showing how [starting material] [eventually returns to / completes a cycle].
```

---

### Overview

```
Overall, the process consists of [X] main stages, beginning with [first stage] and culminating in [final product / output].
/ The cycle begins with [A] and, after [X] distinct stages, returns to the original starting point.
```

---

### Body 1（前半程步骤）

```
In the first stage, [material/subject] is [verb: collected / heated / combined with X]. 
This is then [followed by / subsequently] [second action], where [description]. 
Following this, the [material] undergoes [third process], producing [intermediate output].
```

---

### Body 2（后半程 → 结果）

```
In the [fourth / penultimate] stage, [material] is [action]. 
Subsequently, [final transformation occurs], and the [final product] is [obtained / distributed / released].
/ At this point, the cycle is complete, and the process returns to the initial stage.
```

---

### 流程图常用被动句式

```
is collected from...       is fed into...
is heated to [X°C]        is converted into...
is passed through...       is separated into...
is mixed with...           is released into...
is treated with...         is distributed to...
```

## ✦ 模板七：多图题 / Multiple Charts

> **核心逻辑**：先各自概括 → 再找两图之间的关联 / 对比

### Introduction

**（两图协同）**
```
The [chart type 1] and [chart type 2] provide information about [topic 1] and [topic 2] respectively, 
offering a comprehensive picture of [overall theme].
```

**（两图对比）**
```
The charts outline [topic], with the [first chart] focusing on [aspect A] while the [second] 
examines [aspect B] in [year/region].
```

---

### Overview

```
Overall, [main finding from chart 1], while [main finding from chart 2]. 
The two charts together suggest that [brief cross-chart insight].
```

---

### Body 1（第一张图）

```
According to the [first chart], [A] accounted for the largest proportion at [X%], 
followed by [B] at [Y%]. [C] represented the smallest share, at just [Z%].
```

---

### Body 2（第二张图 + 关联描述）

```
Turning to the [table / second chart], [finding]. This aligns with / contrasts with 
the pattern seen in the [pie chart / bar chart], suggesting that [cross-chart observation].
```

## ✦ 快速套用 — 连接词 & 过渡词速查

| 功能 | 可用表达 |
|------|----------|
| 引出新类别 | Regarding / In terms of / Turning to / As for / With respect to |
| 对比 | In contrast / By contrast / Conversely / While / Whereas |
| 相似 | Similarly / Likewise / Comparably |
| 补充 | Furthermore / In addition / Moreover |
| 例外 | A notable exception was... / Unusually, ... |
| 总结 | Overall / In general / On the whole |
| 时间推进 | Subsequently / In the following years / By [year] / Over the same period |

---

## ✦ 万用 Overview 句型（填空版）

```
Overall, [最大趋势描述], while [对比趋势]. The most notable feature is [极端特征 or 反超点].
```

**示例组合：**

> Overall, the consumption of oil rose steadily throughout the period, while coal experienced a sharp decline. The most notable feature is that natural gas overtook coal as the second most used fuel by 2005.

> Overall, the majority of expenditure was directed towards food and housing, which together accounted for over half of all spending. By contrast, entertainment and clothing represented the smallest shares.
